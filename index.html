Q: What is Palindrome ?

Ans : Palindromes are word games that read the same forwards and backwards. They offer a delightful linguistic challenge and a touch of clever fun in everyday language. Whether it's "racecar," "level," or "never odd or even," palindromes are a source of amusement for those who appreciate the beauty of symmetry in words.
